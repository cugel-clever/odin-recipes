Lasagna Extravaganza

A delicious lasagna for all occasions!

This recipe is fairly simple to implement.

Preparation: 20 minutes.

Cooking time: 70 minutes

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 16 lasagna noodles
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 clove garkic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon parsley flakes
  • 1 teaspook dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper, as per taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups (500 g.) cottage cheese
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese flakes
  • 4 cups of basilic tomato sauce for pasta

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Put 8 liters of water in a large pot and bring to a boil. Add lasagna and cook for ~10 minutes ou until al dente. Drain and put aside.
  3. Place ground beef, garlic, parsley, oregano and salt and pepper in a pan lightly greased with olive oil on medium heat; cook and stir until the beef is golden brown (~15 minutes).
  4. Mix cottage cheese, parmesan cheese and eggs in a medium size bowl until a smooth mixture is obtained. Keep some parmesan cheese for the next step.
  5. Using a glass baking pan appropriately sized, grease the surface including the sides. Lay 4 noodles side by side on the bottom. cover the area with the groud beef mixture. Add the cheese layer. Add a thin layer of sauce. Repeat the procedure 3 other times. Cover the dish with an aluminium foil.
  6. Bake in the oven until the lasagna is bubbling (~30 min.). Remove foil cook and until the parmesan cover is gratiné (~15 min.). Allow 10 minutes for it to stand. Serve with green vegetables and red wine. Enjoy!
